# Project Ideation

## Audiences:
- MTG players
- College students
- CS students
- Gym-goers
- People who like to cook
- People who walk to campus
- People who play video games

## Needs: MTG Players
1. Spend less money
2. Find cheap cards for decks
3. Get new deck ideas
4. Learn about new sets/formats
5. Collection tracking/management
6. Finding people to play with
7. Keeping track of gamestate/boardstate
8. Discussion of cards
9. Trading
10. Art appreciation/creation

## Project Ideas:
- **Boardstate tracker - Winner**
    - Token management (7) - scryfall api for tokens
    - Life total tracker (7)
    - Commander damage/poison counters tracker (7)
    - +1/+1 counter tracker for creature tokens (7)
    - Saving token-sets for specific decklists (7) - firestore for saving
    - Multiple player states(?) (7)
- MTG thesaurus
    - Looks for alternative cards based on oracle text (2)
    - Compare prices (2/8)
    - Crowd-sourced rating system for unpopular cards/Commenting (2/8)
- Pack opening simulator
    - Allows for practice for draft/sealed events (1/4)
    - Potentially with future sets once spoilers are out (4)
    - Gambling (1)
    - Saving collection to a profile (5)
    - View other profiles (6)
